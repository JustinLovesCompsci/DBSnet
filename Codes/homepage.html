DBS Alumni Network

DBSnet helps connect the most talented, driven and business-minded Duke students and alumni together. On the "Table" page, you may find personal information of DBS members and alumni, such as their past internship experiences, contact details and academic achievements. You may also discuss ideas with each other on the forum, either about networking or entrepreneurship.
